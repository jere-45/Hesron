Wau-Bulolo Valley

The Golden Valley

The Bulolo valley was the site of one of the world's largest gold rushes in the 1920s. Today, it is known for its coffee, timber, and the incredible Saruwaged mountains.

Highlights: Visit the Wau Ecology Institute, explore the McAdam National Park, and learn about the historic gold dredges that still sit in the valley.
